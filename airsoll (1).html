01 / OIL-INJECTED SCREW

Belt & Direct-Driven Screw Compressors

Twin helical rotors compress air continuously rather than in pulses, which is why screw units are the default for anything running more than a shift a day. Choose belt-drive for flexible output staging, or direct-drive for a tighter footprint and fewer moving parts to service.

02 / PM VSD

Permanent-Magnet VSD Energy-Saving Compressors

A fixed-speed compressor either runs at full output or unloads — both waste power. Our PM VSD range uses a permanent-magnet motor and inverter to match motor speed to the plant's actual demand curve, second by second, which is where most of the electricity bill savings come from.

Power range
7.5–160 kW
Motor class

03 / OIL-FREE SCREW

Certified Oil-Free Screw Compressors

Where compressed air touches the product — inhalers, tablets, wafers, packaged food — a single carry-over droplet is a contamination event, not a maintenance note. These units use water- or air-injected rotor sets with zero lubricating oil in the compression chamber, tested and certified to Class 0.

11–160 kW
Air purity

06 / TREATMENT

Refrigerated Dryers, Line Filters & Air Receivers

We size dryers, coalescing/particulate filters and receiver tanks as one matched set, not separate afterthoughts.

How we build

Four stages, in order, every time.

No stage starts until the one before it has signed off — that's the whole method.

01 — DESIGN

Spec to the duty cycle

We size the airend, motor and treatment train to your actual run hours and ambient conditions, not a catalogue default.

02 — BUILD

Assemble on a fixed line

Every unit is built on a numbered line with torque and pressure checks logged at each station, not sampled.

03 — TEST

Run at rated load before it ships

Each machine runs a full load-and-leak test on-site here, and the test sheet ships with the unit.

04 — SUPPORT

Stay reachable after delivery

Commissioning support and a stocked parts line for the life of the machine, not just the warranty period.
